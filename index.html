Who Am I?

THIBAUT, Project Manager

Hi! I'm Thibaut Grenier, a French visual designer currently living in Metz, France. Humble open-minded explorer and analytic mind, I like to solve complex problems and find solutions “by design”. Detail-oriented, I'll take the best from productivity tools to make the most of my free time!

I've created "La Recyclerie du Sport Lorraine" back in France in 2022, a non-profit oganization helping people to get sports equipment and reducing waste.

✔ Web design/project manager bachelor degree from the Tech/Business campus Epitech Digital, with 6+ years of experience (mid-senior level) as a designer whose 5 years as a digital product manager
